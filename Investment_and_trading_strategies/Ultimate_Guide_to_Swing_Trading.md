### Swing Trading Strategy Steps:

**Step 1: Finding Stocks (Scanning)**
The speaker uses a specific scanner template during the middle of the day (after 11:00 a.m. ET) to identify potential setups. The criteria include: (21:29 - 23:05)
*   **Market Cap:** Greater than $1 billion.
*   **Share Price:** Greater than $1.
*   **Volume:** At least 500,000 shares traded by midday.
*   **Performance:** Must be up at least 3% on the day.

**Step 2: Technical and Chart Pattern Analysis**
Once a list of candidates is generated, the trader evaluates them against specific indicators and patterns: (23:46 - 25:56)
*   **Moving Averages:** The stock should be trading above the **200 SMA** (long-term trend) and trending/hugging the **8 EMA** (short-term momentum).
*   **Reclamation:** If the stock recently broke down, it should demonstrate a history of reclaiming the 8 EMA.
*   **Chart Patterns:** Look for consolidation or potential breakouts from established daily resistance levels.

**Step 3: Execution (Entries and Exits)**
*   **Entry:** The goal is to enter as close to the 8 EMA as possible, rather than chasing a breakout at the peak. (26:28 - 27:49)
*   **Exits:** The speaker suggests selling in partial sizes (quarters). A portion is sold into the breakout to take profit, while half is kept to continue trailing the trend. (28:01 - 28:36)
*   **Stop Loss:** The stop is placed below the 8 EMA; if the stock closes below this line, the position is closed. (28:36 - 28:50)
